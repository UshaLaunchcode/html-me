My Road Trip To Boston

The follwing are the places my family and I are visited on our trip to Boston.

Niagara Falls

The first place we visited on our trip is Niagara Falls which is located in Buffalo, New York. We were all amazed to see such huge natural water falls.Our favorite attractions were Cave Of Winds & Maid Of The Mist!

Boston

Next was our final destination Boston, Massachusetts.We came to visit our family and had a very good time with them. Some of the things we did there includes going jetsking, going on a Duck Tour, and visiting MIT University.

New York

Next stop on our return trip was once again New York.This time we took a cruise to see the Statue Of Liberty. We enjoyed the cruise and the good view of the statue. We were also able to see the Time Square and had a fun time there as well.

Saint Louis

Finally, we returned home safely.Home Sweet Home!!
